
Given a string  **_s_**  , find the length of the longest substring **_t_** that contains  **at most** 2 distinct characters.

**Example 1:**

**Input:** "eceba"
**Output:** 3
**Explanation: _t_**  is "ece" which its length is 3.

**Example 2:**

**Input:** "ccaabbb"
**Output:** 5
**Explanation: _t_**  is "aabbb" which its length is 5.
